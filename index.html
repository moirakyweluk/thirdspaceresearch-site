Case Studies

A few examples of how we translate research into decisions and outcomes.

Biopharma • Rare Disease

De-risking value proposition for a novel diagnostic

A biopharma partner needed clarity on HCP adoption drivers for a first-in-class diagnostic. We combined expert interviews, payer landscape scans, and iterative message testing.

  • Challenge: Unclear decision pathways across specialties
  • Approach: Mixed-methods research + claims scan
  • Output: Evidence-ready positioning & claims map
3× faster alignment+28% intent to adopt
Digital Health • Behavior Change

Improving onboarding to boost 90-day retention

A chronic-care app saw early churn. We mapped patient journeys and tested onboarding concepts to reduce cognitive load and align expectations.

  • Challenge: Low activation after day 7
  • Approach: Journey mapping + prototype testing
  • Output: Onboarding scripts & risk-minimization micro-content
+35% day-30 activation+18 pts NPS
MedTech • Provider Workflow

Positioning a device within crowded OR workflows

We clarified use cases and operational barriers across surgeons, scrub techs, and hospital admin to shape GTM and training assets.

  • Challenge: Competing priorities in the OR
  • Approach: Contextual inquiry + claims clarity
  • Output: Role-specific training + adoption playbook
−22% setup time+40% trial-to-adopt
Payer & Systems • Education

We designed plain-language education for patients & HCPs, aligning with regulatory guidance while fitting busy clinic reality.

  • Challenge: Low comprehension of REMS materials
  • Approach: Co-design + readability testing
  • Output: Multi-channel, role-based materials
